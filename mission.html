Mission

Our mission at CoderSpace is to work in teams to design and build websites for local clients while gaining real world experience in web development, teamwork and leadership. Together as a team we would like to succesfully complete a responsive website for a real-world client. We will also like to further develop our leadership, team-working, and presentation skills. We believe that "teamwork makes the dream work". Furthermore the CodePanthers would like the further their knowledge in several coding languages including HTML, CSS, and JAVASCRIPT.
