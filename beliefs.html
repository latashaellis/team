Beliefs

Code Panthers is a ground made up of 4 young leaders. We are young inventors building upon our techology experience. We belive that coding is a different way to express diffent personalities, based off a webpage. In order to be completely succesful in all tasks we attempt, we believe that having a solid foundation in teamwork and leadership skill is the key to suceess. Leadership is not being in control but influencing the whole team to be better. We believe that teamwork is working together and pushing each other to do better. By having skills in our own diffrent ways we can be one of the greatest teams out there.
